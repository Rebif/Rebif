STUDY DESIGN: To assess the effects of exposure to Rebif® on long-term efficacy outcomes, patients in the original PRISMS-2 study were invited to a single follow-up visit 15 years after initial randomization. Clinical outcomes over 15 years were compared according to cumulative dose exposure groups and according to cumulative time receiving Rebif® from baseline to 15 years (post hoc analyses). This study was a single-visit follow-up and analyses were exploratory; results should be interpreted with caution. The design of this study does not allow for definitive statements regarding causality.
